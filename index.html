Experiences

Education

I attended Portland State University from 1998 to 2005. When I left, I had a bachelor's degree in Contemporary Literature and a master's degree in Education. I studied at Beijing University in August of 2005 where I became certified to teach Teach Oral English as a Foregin Language (TOEFL) in China.

Work Experience

I began my career in education as a middle school language arts teacher, but quickly shifted into teaching spoken English and grammer. Blending reading and writing with English language instruction provided me with many opportunities, taking me across Asia and America before returning home to Portland.

Why Programming?

My first computer was a cast off Apple 2e, as my father was an early adopter of home computers. Over the years, I jumped back and forth between operating systems, always proficiently using, but rarely understanding. I made attempts, but never had the opportunity for direct instruction, back at the dawn of the internet, when seeing a picture through the phone line felt like magic. I have always been interested in the inner workings of computers, moreso as they have become more powerful and more ubiquitous, but it never felt accessible to me until now. If you have time, take a look at my portfolio to the right. It's all very basic, because I'm just getting started.
